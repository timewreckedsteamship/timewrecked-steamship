Timewrecked Steamship

It's a steampunk camping event -- but not merely so.
It's a blend of steampunk convention, Maker Faire, and Burning Man
Really, it will be what you make it.

Crew/passengers/stow-aways are encouraged to bring whatever mad science you have wrought, as we do not know what opportunities for trade or conquest we may discover together.

Programming, the likes of "how a steam engine works" and, of course, croquet and tea, is merely pageantry leading up to our maiden voyage exploring the unknown vastness.

Some of our engineers have cautioned that activating the time-engine will cause it to catch fire and suspect it will either malfunction or suck us uncontrolled into another time. But we are proceeding heedless in the spirit of adventure.

To help scout a location that is both steam and burn friendly, to help construct the time-engine, to suggest or present a program, or to volunteer in any other way, please email volunteer [at] timewreckedsteamship.com

- Capt. Ephemeral
